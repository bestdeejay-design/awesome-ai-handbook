# Content Quality and Security Policy

## Our Commitment

This handbook is a community-driven collection of articles, references, and best practices about AI, models, and agents. We strive to maintain accurate, up-to-date, and safe content for all users.

## Reporting Content Issues

If you find any of the following, please let us know:

* **Factual errors** or outdated information
* **Broken links** (external or internal)
* **Copyright-infringing material**
* **Offensive, misleading, or harmful content**
* **Missing citations** or proper attributions

Please report these issues via:

* **Email**: **[YOUR_EMAIL@EXAMPLE.COM]**
* **GitHub Issues**: [Create a new issue](https://github.com/bestdeejay-design/awesome-ai-handbook/issues) (please use the label "content issue")

When reporting, include:
- The exact location (file name, section, or URL)
- A clear description of the problem
- If possible, a suggested correction or replacement

## Response Time

We will acknowledge your report within **48 hours** and aim to resolve content issues within **5 business days**.

## Content Moderation

We follow the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) for all contributions. Any content that violates our community guidelines will be reviewed and may be removed or revised.

## Preferred Languages

We accept reports in **English** and **Russian**.

## How You Can Help

You can also directly contribute fixes by submitting a pull request – see our [Contributing Guide](CONTRIBUTING.md).
